I worked on a startup with a friend in 2019 - we called the project [Pegasus](https://ageofempires.fandom.com/wiki/Pegasus). We worked for one of WA's largest property groups to try and measure the foot traffic going through and around two of their stores. It was a lot of fun, and a lot of work. We're not working on it any more for various reasons, but I wanted to write a post to share some of the cool things I worked on around video processing with deep learning for large data sets (order TB).
