Rainy hike around the Mundaring Weir! There are heaps of hikes in the area. We went along sections of the [Munda Biddi](https://www.mundabiddi.org.au/) and the [Bibbulmun](https://www.bibbulmuntrack.org.au/) tracks. Finished off with a visit to the old Mundaring Weir Hotel. Bring a change of clothes if you're going for a hike in the rain :)
